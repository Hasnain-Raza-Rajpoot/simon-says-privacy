Privacy Policy

Simon Says: AI Pose Game

Last Updated: September 10, 2025
Quick Summary: Our app uses your camera only for real-time pose detection during gameplay. No images, videos, or personal data are stored, shared, or transmitted. Everything happens locally on your device.

1. Information We Collect

Simon Says: AI Pose Game is designed with privacy in mind. Here's what we do and don't collect:

What We DO Use:

  • Camera Access: Used exclusively for real-time pose detection during gameplay
  • Temporary Image Processing: Camera frames are processed locally on your device for pose recognition

What We DON'T Collect or Store:

  • No personal images or videos are saved
  • No biometric data is stored or transmitted
  • No personal information (names, emails, phone numbers)
  • No user accounts or profiles are created
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers

2. How We Use Your Information

The camera feed is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Real-time Pose Detection: To recognize body movements and poses during gameplay
  • Game Functionality: To determine if players are correctly following "Simon Says" commands
  • Local Processing Only: All analysis happens on your device - nothing is sent to our servers

3. Data Storage and Sharing

We store NO data:
  • Camera frames are processed and immediately discarded
  • No user data is stored on our servers
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No analytics or tracking services are used

4. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed to be family-friendly and safe for children:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • No user profiles or accounts are created for any age group
  • We recommend parental supervision during camera use
  • Parents should ensure children have adequate, safe space for physical movement

5. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your privacy:

  • All processing occurs locally on your device
  • No network transmission of camera data
  • Camera access is only active during gameplay
  • App permissions are clearly defined and limited

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your privacy:

  • Camera Permission: You can revoke camera access at any time through your device settings
  • Data Deletion: Since we don't store data, there's nothing to delete
  • App Removal: Simply uninstalling the app removes all associated functionality

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically.

8. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party technology:

  • TensorFlow Lite: For AI pose detection (processing occurs locally on device)
  • Flutter Framework: For app development (no data collection)

No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are integrated into our app.
